Crypto country is a country with its own AnonOracle within Anonutopia network and its own associated tag.

Universal basic income is profit made by Anonutopia's services and applications in some country split between the citizens of that country (and Anonutopia). AnonOracles are the foundations of our infrastructure in every country. This is because we want Anonutopia to be completely decentralized and redundant.
